LaithAlayassa.com

When I became a resident assistant in college, I wanted to share knowledge and guidance with my residents in a modern format, after much thought, I initiated a weekly newsletter.

It contains bits of interesting things I find during the week, from useful advice I got, to practical philosophy I read, to excerpts from fascinating books.

The feedback was overwhelmingly positive, and non-residents started asking to also receive the letter, so I turned it into a website and shared it with the world.
